Archive

This page documents the bi-weekly research discussions held during the Winter Term 2024/2025. Sessions took place on Fridays from 14:00 to 15:00, from October 2024 to January 2025.

Discussion Format

Each session followed this schedule:

  • 14:00 - 14:10: Ice-breaker (Discussion of current hot research topics)
  • 14:10 - 14:40: Research Presentation
  • 14:40 - 15:00: Q&A / Open Discussion
